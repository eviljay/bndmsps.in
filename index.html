ABOUT US

This is a self financing college of teacher education Baba Narayan Das Mahila Shikshak Prashikshan Sansthan has been founded by Indian education & development society Neem ka Thana , Dist: Sikar (Rajasthan). Indian education & development society is a voluntary non-profit organization registered under Raj society registration Act 1958 sec.28 in the year 1999. Baba Narayan Das Mahila Shikshak Prashikshan Sansthan is a private no granted organization will be working on the principal of self-financing. Necessary infrastructure facilities and initial financial support has been provided by the foundation society.
